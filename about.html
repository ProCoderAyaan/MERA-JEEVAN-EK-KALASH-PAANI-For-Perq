About Us

About Us

Mera Jeevan Ek Kalash Paani is a non-governmental organization established by Shri Dattatrey Sidam, a retired Assistant Commissioner of Police from Mumbai. Through his own experience as a growing kid in a tribal community and during the four decades of police service in Maharashtra, he realised that inspite of governmental efforts there remain significant gaps when it comes to opportunities and development in rural and tribal areas. This personal experience and compassion drove him to try and improve the lot of such underprivileged communities.

Since our establishment in 2014, we have focused on promoting education for underprivileged poor children, raising healthcare awareness, and fostering environmental sustainability. Through our holistic approach, we aim to create empowered, self-reliant communities.

Mission

To bridge gaps in education, healthcare, & eco-awareness, transforming underserved rural areas into thriving ecosystems.

Our Vision

A future where every child is educated, every community is healthy, and every individual is a beacon of environment-friendly practices.

Track Record of Success

Mera Jeevan Ek Kalash Paani has successfully impacted over thousands of families across multiple rural/tribal districts in Maharashtra, with tangible improvements in education, healthcare, and environmental practices. Our campaigns and workshops have empowered individuals, fostered sustainable growth, and nurtured resilience within socially & economically backward communities.

Transparency and Accountability

We pride ourselves on maintaining the highest standards of integrity and transparency in utilizing charity receipts. From time to time, we also tie up with leading celebrities and performing artists to help in fund raising for our charitable initiatives.
